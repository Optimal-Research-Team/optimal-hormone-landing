How it works
A real program. Not a single appointment.

The Optimal Hormone Program is structured over 12 weeks with a dedicated care team — so nothing falls through the cracks and you're never wondering what comes next.

1

Free intro call

Speak with our care coordinator. She'll walk you through the program, ask a few questions to confirm it's a good fit, and book your first visit — no commitment until you're ready.

2

Onboarding & testing

Your first in-clinic visit includes a body composition scan (InBody), a program orientation, and your OHIP-covered bloodwork requisition for a comprehensive hormone and metabolic panel.

Estradiol, testosterone, progesterone, FSH, TSH, thyroid, cortisol, DHEA — and more. All covered by OHIP.

3

60-minute NP consultation

A full hour with your Nurse Practitioner — not ten minutes. She reviews your bloodwork, your symptoms, your history, and builds a personalized treatment plan. If hormone therapy is right for you, she writes the prescription that same day.

4

Care coordinator check-ins

Between visits, your care coordinator checks in at week one and week three to make sure things are on track and answer any questions as your treatment begins.

5

Two NP follow-ups

Structured follow-ups to review your progress, adjust your treatment, and repeat your body composition scan at 12 weeks — so you can see the change, not just feel it.

6

Ongoing care

At discharge, your prescriptions are renewed for one year. Annual follow-ups and as-needed visits are available. Your family doctor receives a full report after every clinical visit.
