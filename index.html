The Program

A service created for women experiencing perimenopause or menopause looking for an attentive experience where you feel heard and your perspective is valued. Patients work one-on-one with a Nurse Practitioner who specializes in women's hormone health — who will listen, review labs in depth, and build a personalized plan.

The program includes a comprehensive hormonal assessment, body composition analysis, and OHIP-covered bloodwork during an initial 60-minute consultation with two follow-up visits and ongoing access to the care team through our secure patient portal.

The program is ideal for women who are experiencing symptoms consistent with perimenopause or menopause, such as:

  • Hot flashes, night sweats, or sleep disruption
  • Brain fog, mood changes, or fatigue
  • Low libido or vaginal dryness
  • Irregular periods or other hormonal symptoms
Not sure if you qualify? The intake call with our care coordinator includes a brief verbal questionnaire to confirm fit — before any payment is made.

No. You do not need a family physician to enroll. If you don't have a GP, our NP will explain how any abnormal lab results are handled at your onboarding appointment. At the end of the program, your prescriptions can be renewed for one year directly through our clinic.

Getting Started

The first step is a brief phone call with our care coordinator. They'll walk you through the program, ask a few questions to confirm you're a good fit, and — if everything looks right — book your first in-clinic appointment.

After the call, you'll receive a short online questionnaire to complete before your first visit. No commitment is required until your onboarding appointment.

Your first clinic visit is the Onboarding & Testing Appointment. Here's what to expect:

  • A warm welcome at the front desk, followed by time in our lounge
  • A 30-minute orientation with your care coordinator covering how the program works
  • An InBody body composition scan (∼5 minutes)
  • Your OHIP bloodwork requisition to take to LifeLabs or Dynacare at your convenience
  • Payment is processed at this appointment

Your initial consultation with the NP is scheduled after your bloodwork results are returned.

Bloodwork & Testing

Yes — all baseline bloodwork ordered through this program is OHIP-covered. Your NP prepares the requisition, and you take it to a LifeLabs or Dynacare near you.

The panel includes a comprehensive hormone assessment:

  • Estradiol & Estriol
  • Free & Total Testosterone
  • Progesterone & 17-OH Progesterone
  • FSH, TSH, Free T3, Free T4
  • DHEAS & DHEA
  • AM Cortisol

For some patients, the NP may recommend additional testing if standard labs are inconclusive or a more detailed picture would help. These are optional and out-of-pocket:

  • Mira Home Hormone Testing — an at-home urine-based device for monitoring hormonal patterns. We can provide a discount code.
  • DUTCH Test — a detailed metabolite-level hormone analysis for complex cases. Typically $300–450. Suggested only when clinically warranted.
  • SHBG - A blood test that can be done with base line bloodwork but is not OHIP covered. Can be helpful in interpretting testosterone levels.
Your Appointments

This is the primary clinical appointment — 60 minutes, one-on-one with your Nurse Practitioner. It takes place at least one week after your bloodwork is completed, giving the NP time to review everything in advance.

During the visit:

  • Full symptom assessment
  • Detailed review and explanation of your lab results
  • Personalized treatment plan
  • Prescriptions and/or nutraceutical recommendations as appropriate

After the visit, you'll receive your lab results and a written treatment plan summary through our secure patient portal. A note will also be sent to your family physician if you have one.

Your program includes two follow-up appointments after your initial consultation:

  • Follow-Up #1 (approx. 8–12 weeks post-consult) — Virtual or in-person, 30 minutes. Symptom check, dose review, and any adjustments needed.
  • Follow-Up #2 (approx. 8–12 weeks after Follow-Up #1) — Recommended in-person, 30 minutes. Includes a repeat InBody body composition scan. At the end of this visit, you're discharged from the structured program with prescriptions renewed for one year.

After the program, annual follow-ups and as-needed visits are available.

Yes. Follow-Up #1 can be done virtually or in-person. Follow-Up #2 is recommended in-person, as it includes your second InBody body composition scan to measure changes over the course of the program.

Prescriptions & Ongoing Care

If your NP recommends hormone therapy, your prescription will be sent to a compounding pharmacy. Please allow 3–7 business days for compounded prescriptions to be prepared — the pharmacy will contact you when it's ready.

At your intake appointment, we'll confirm your pharmacy preferences and document them in your chart.

All program patients have access to our secure patient portal with messaging enabled throughout the program. You can reach your care team with questions and receive your lab results and treatment plan documents there as well.

Your safety is the top priority. If you experience any of the following, please go to your nearest Emergency Room and notify our clinic:

  • Severe or sudden headaches
  • Calf pain or swelling
  • Chest pain
  • Persistent heavy bleeding
  • Significant mood deterioration

You'll receive a written escalation protocol handout at your initial consultation. Our care team is also reachable through the portal during business hours.
